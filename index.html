The Problem

A Shopify clothing brand doing 80+ orders per day was losing roughly 65% of customers at checkout. Their only recovery tool was a single generic email sent 24 hours later. Conversion rate on that email: under 1%. The founder was manually following up on high-value carts via WhatsApp — unsustainable and reactive.

The Solution

Built a fully automated AI cart recovery system. Within 15 minutes of abandonment, a personalised WhatsApp message goes out — crafted by GPT-4 using the customer's name and exact products. Two follow-ups at strategic intervals. Conversational objection handling. Zero human involvement at any step. Runs continuously 24/7.

The Problem

A B2B services business was receiving 30–50 enquiries per day. Every one required manual reading, categorisation, CRM entry, and follow-up — consuming 5+ hours of the team's day. Hot leads were going cold by the time they were contacted. Quality was inconsistent. Errors were frequent. The team felt buried.

The Solution

Built an automated lead pipeline that processes every incoming enquiry within 60 seconds. AI reads the message, scores lead quality (Hot / Warm / Cold with reasoning), auto-populates the CRM, and triggers the right follow-up action — an email, a Slack alert to sales, or a WhatsApp message. The team only deals with leads that are ready to close.

🛡 Fallback systems — why they're non-negotiable

WhatsApp goes down. Shopify has outages. CRMs timeout. Without a fallback, your automation silently fails and you lose data. I build every system assuming external services will fail — so your automation keeps running even when they do.

📋 Logging — full visibility, always

You can't manage what you can't see. Every automation I build writes a timestamped log of every action — what ran, when, whether it succeeded, and what failed. Bugs get caught fast. Performance is measurable. You always have a full audit trail.

✅ Validation — bad data doesn't break the system

Real-world data is messy — missing fields, invalid formats, edge cases. Before any action runs, data is validated and cleaned. This prevents crashes, incorrect actions, and the kind of errors that embarrass a business in front of their customers.
